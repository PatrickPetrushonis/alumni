News from the Nursing Almanac 2016

Great news for the Bridgeport Hospital School of Nursing. They are being recognized by the Nursing Schools Almanac 2016 ranking BHSN as one of the best nursing school.

BHSN is being ranked as one of the top nursing programs; even in our rank of #31 - all schools before us are universities with multiple advanced programs and there is no other 2 year program ahead of us in the New England States and only one in the US ranking of all vocational 2-year programs (approx. 1900 and we're #2). This is outstanding and Alumnae Members should be proud. Many thanks to our faculty and staff at BHSN.
