Guidelines for:
The Bridgeport Hospital School of Nursing Alumnae Association I, Inc.
Nursing Education Scholarship

  1. A recipient must be a current member of the BHSN Alumnae Association I, Inc. for at least one year.
  2. Scholarships are awarded only for degrees in nursing.
  3. A maximum amount of $4000.00 may be awarded to each recipient.
  4. Scholarship money will be awarded upon: a. Proof of successful completion of the course (grade of C or better) by providing an official sealed transcript and b. Submission of university/college billing statement.
  5. Documentation of other scholarships and/or company reimbursement for the course being taken must be provided.
  6. Applications for the Nursing Education Scholarship must be submitted from January 15th through March 15th.
  7. Scholarship monies are awarded at the Bridgeport Hospital School of Nursing Alumnae Association I, Inc. Annual April Meeting/Banquet.
  8. Eligible courses are those taken during the year prior to or following the Annual April Meeting/Banquet.
  9. The amount of scholarships to be awarded each year is to be decided by the board of the BHSN Alumnae Association I, Inc.
  10. In the event of more applications being received than the amount to be given, the names of the applicants will be blinded out and read by a minimum of 3 readers, preferably 5 readers for selection of the recipients.
  11. Scholarships will be based on financial need and professional goals as presented in the application paragraph.

Scholarship committee: Caren Silhavey (chairman), Edi Poidomani, Linda Podolak, Dottie Connelly, and Cathy Nucera.
