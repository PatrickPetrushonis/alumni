Our annual Spring Banquet/Meeting will be again @ Paris/Rome Restaurant, 595 Main St., Stratford, next to the Sikorsky Memorial Airport/ across from the old Textron Lycoming on Tuesday, April 12, 2005. Our 100th Anniversary of the Alumnae is nearly upon us in 2006. We need your help with a place and time to hold this gala event. If this should be formal/ or a Luncheon, with a tour of the Bridgeport School of Nursing or the Hospital ? Also, if you have anything that you can share for the event such as: memorabilia from the early 1900’s, photos, medical instruments, yearbooks, journals, newspaper clippings, uniforms, caps, experiences that we could put in a keepsake book, copies of diplomas, etc.(what ever you can think of). If you have any input, please call one of the board members.

A ride list is being compiled for those members that need a ride to one of our April or September meetings. If you need a ride or can drive someone, please contact one of the board members.
