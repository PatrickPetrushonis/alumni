Dear Members,

Our Annual Spring Meeting/Dinner will be held on Tuesday, April 8, 2008, 6:00PM, at TESTO’S Restaurant (formerly the Three Doors Restaurant), 1775 Madison Ave, Bridgeport, Ct. Directions are included. Entrees will be (1)Veal Parmesan, (2) Broiled Salmon, or (3) Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast, pasta, salad, potato and vegetable, coffee, tea, and dessert,and gratuities all included

for $24.60 per person. Please send meal choice and checks by 4/4/08.

The Speaker for the evening will be Linda Strong, Nursing Professor @ Sacred Heart University on her experiences and public health issues that arose in certain areas of the South, post Katrina. Ceu’s will be awarded after the presentation.

The National Student Nurses Association will hold their annual convention in San Antonio, Texas. AAI will donate $85 for each of the 2 students and 4 NSNA officers from BHSN, to attend.

The Accelerated BHSN program graduated 37 nurses in December, 2007. Thirty-one have joined the Alumnae. Gold pins for the top 2 scholar achievers will be awarded in May, 2008 at the combined graduation ceremony for the 12/07 & 5/08 graduates.
