The Speaker for the evening to be announced.

We celebrated our 100th Anniversary of our Alumnae being incorporated in 1906.This gala celebration

took place on Saturday, September 23, 2006 at the Holiday Inn, Bridgeport, Ct and was attended by more

than 100 alumnae and guests. AAI members traveled from as far as California, Washington State, New Hampshire

and Georgia to attend.One of our youngest of heart Life Members, Irene Firmender,cl’38,also attended.Bob Trefry,

CEOof BH,Carol DeBlois,Director of BHSN, and Steve Jacobs, President of the BH Foundation, also attended A

tour of Bridgeport Hospital and the School of Nursing with a display of memorabilia followed on Sunday after a

gracious brunch in our honor given by the Bridgeport Hospital and School Administration. A program booklet was

compiled with the history of our Alumnae,nostalgic pictures, a synopsis of some of our members and their successes,

our bylaws, and our past presidents. Gold and silver charms were available for order with the 100th Anniversary

engraving. We will have a collage of pictures available at the dinner for everyone to view and have extra booklets

for anyone interested. We would like to thank our committees and Committee Chair People- Caren Silhavey,cl’69,

Cathy Nucera,cl’72, Beverly Tamas,cl’52, Elaine Mileski,cl’54, Mary Toohey-Repko,cl’80 and Debi Petrushonis,

cl’77, for their dedication and hard work in putting this successful event together! Check out our Web Page!

The National Student Nurses Association will hold their annual convention in California and the AAI will

pay the entrance fee of $85 for each of the 7 BHSN board members to attend. Congratulations to the student nurses

for obtaining the NSNA board positions! A Tea for Alumnae will be given on 4/26/07 in the BHSN Lounge.
