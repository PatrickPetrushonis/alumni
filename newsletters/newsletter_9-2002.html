Bridgeport Hospital School of Nursing Alumnae Association I

September, 2002

Dear Members,

We hope to find all of you enjoying the remnants of this long hot Summer, relaxing and staying cool! In planning our Fall Banquet/Alumnae meeting, we again chose Stella’s Restaurant, Oranoque Plaza, 7365 Main St., Stratford, across from Sikorsky’s, for Tuesday, Sept., 10, 2002 @ 6:00P.M.,dinner at 6:30 in the banquet hall next to the restaurant. Dinner choices are:Stuffed Filet of Sole,Chicken Piccata, or Roast Sirloin. Also included is ziti, toss salad, potato,fresh vegetable,bread/butter,desert, coffee, and tea. Total price is $23.00, tax and tip included. A cash bar will also be available.

The speaker for the evening is pending.

At our April meeting, we elected the same officers for the next 2 years, plus 2 new board members: Dorothy Connelly and Barbara Kulikowski. Welcome and congratulations!

Graduation luncheon was held on 4/25/02 with a catered lunch from Vazzy’s inwhich we used a $150 refund from one of our last banquets. Beverly Tamas, AA2 President, & Mary Toohey-Repko,AAI President spoke to the new grads about the Alumnaes and membership.

Graduation was held @ the First Presbyterian Church in Fairfield, on May 7, 2002 with 56 graduates. There were 46 grads that took and passed the Boards. Congratulations !

The SCL award was presented to Jennifer Delvecchio and the MCL award went to Frances Revels. They received gold pins from AAI. Congratulations to the graduates and especially to Jennifer and Frances!!!

The Alumnae Tea was held at the School of Nursing on May 3, 2002 and was given by the students.

The year 2006 is the 100th Anniversary of the BHSN AA. Celebration ideas are requested and a committee needs to be formulated. If anyone has any suggestions, please forward them to any of the board members. Your help would be greatly appreciated. Old pictures,uniforms,medical artifacts!!!

Stephen Jakab, CFRE,President of the BH Foundation,INC, has forwarded AAI information about charitable giving as part of estate planning. The BH Foundation and People’s is offering a free one-hour individualized consultation -call (203)-384-3522 to set up an appointment. More information will be shared at the AAI meeting.

The Class of 1956 is planning a reunion- please contact Diane Mayo, 57 Glend Park Rd, East Orange, NJ,07017 or @(973)-678-2342.

Members of the class of 1985 are asked to contact Nancy Peterson @ (203)-384-3819 with their current addresses,phone numbers and e-mail addresses for a reunion- Nancy can also be reached at BH education dept. for nurses.

Sally Pexa, daughter of Cecilia Rey,cl ‘44, is looking for help for her mom after a recent illness-please call her at (631)-286-1978 if you can help or know of someone. Thanks.
