Dear AAI Members, March, 2010

Our Annual spring Meeting/Dinner will be held on Tues, April, 13, 2010@ 6:00PM, at TESTO’S

Restaurant, 1775 Madison Ave, Bridgeport, Ct. Entrees will be (1)Chicken with mushroom, prosuitto and cheese, (2) Eggplant parmesan or (3) Filet of Sole, vegetable, salad, coffee, tea, and dessert with gratuities included. The price is $25.00 per person. Please send meal choice and checks by 4/8 to Cathy Nucera.

The Speaker for the evening will be Dr. Beata Skudlarska, Geriatric Medicine, from Stratford.

If there is a specific topic of interest to you, in Geriatrics, please let one of the board members know before the April dinner. Our slate of Officers of the AAI Board will be presented and voted on at the April dinner.

The Accelerated BHSN program graduated 61 nurses in December, 2009. All new grads joined the Alumnae. We would like to welcome the new graduates to the Alumnae and congratulate them on their success! The Graduation ceremony for the December, 2009 and May, 2010 student nurses, will be held in May, 2010.

The National Student Nurses Association will hold their annual convention this year. The Alumnae has provided the entrance fee for the BH student nurses who attend this event each year.
