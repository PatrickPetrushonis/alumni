BRIDGEPORT HOSPITAL SCHOOL OF NURSING ALUMNAE ASSOC. I

Dear AAI Members, March, 2009

Our Annual spring Meeting/Dinner will be held on Tues, April, 14, 2009 6:00PM, at TESTO’S

Restaurant, 1775 Madison Ave, Bridgeport, Ct. Entrees will be (1)Chicken Marsala, (2) Veal Parmesan, or

(3) Filet of Sole Franchaise(in lemon sauce), pasta, vegetable, salad, coffee, tea, and dessert with gratuities included. The price is $20.00 per person. Please send meal choice and checks by 4/9/09 to Cathy Nucera.

The tentative Speaker for the evening will be Dr. Gary Rader, Chiropractor and Acupuncture Therapist from Stratford. Dr. Rader will discuss the benefits of acupuncture therapy.

The Accelerated BHSN program graduated 48 nurses in December, 2008. Forty five joined the Alumnae. Gold pins for the top 2 scholars will be awarded in May, 2009 at the official graduation ceremony. We would like to welcome the new graduates to the Alumnae and congratulate them on their success! The Graduation ceremony for December,2008, and May,2009 student nurses, will be in May,2009.

The National Student Nurses Association will hold their annual convention in Tennessee this year. The Alumnae has provided the entrance fee for the BH student nurses who attend this event and will await the decision of the students attending this year.

The Student Nurses will hold their annual Alumnae Tea at a date and time to be determined. Final plans are in the process for May, 2009, at the School of Nursing.
