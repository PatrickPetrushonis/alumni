BRIDGEPORT HOSPITAL SCHOOL OF NURSING

ALUMNAE ASSOCIATION 1

Dear Members,

March, 2006

We have entered our 100th year as an Alumnae Association! Our Gala event will take place in September, 2006. More details will be included in this newsletter, on our Web Page, and at our April banquet. The date for our April banquet is Tuesday, April 11, 2006 at 6PM. which will be held at Paris/Rome Restaurant, 595 Main St., Stratford, next to the Sikorsky Airport (across from the old Avco Lycoming Co.) The dinner choices are: Stuffed Fillet of Sole with crab, Chicken Florentine, or Beef Medallions with creamy black peppercorn sauce. Choice of appetizers are: Soup of the day, Fried mozzarella, or Stuffed mushrooms. Pasta, salad, dessert, coffee/tea, tax and tip are included. The price is $27.00 per person.

Last September, we had a Sunday brunch11:30 AM to 2 PM @ Paris/Rome that was just wonderful! All who attended asked to repeat this type of Alumnae banquet for the Fall each year! We are also open to other places of choice that our members would prefer to meet. Contact one of the Board members.

There will be a guest Speaker for the evening from the BHSN, one of the Student Nurses, who has attended a recent National Student Nursing Convention. The Board has agreed to assist the 6 student nurses attending the Baltimore, Maryland, Student Nurse Association Conference with their entrance fees of $80.00 each.

The Students of Bridgeport Hospital School of Nursing wish to hold their annual Alumnae Tea in appreciation for all of the wonderful things that we do for the school and the students on Thursday, April 27, 2006 from12PM to 2PM at the School of Nursing lounge

The Accelerated Nursing Class @ BHSN graduated 37 students in December, 2005. Of these, 36 have filled out applications and joined the Alumnae. Our President, Mary Toohey-Repko attended the ceremony and awarded gold pins to Lisa Lombardi, Summa cum laude and Christopher Sage, Magna cum laude. Congratulations to all the graduates!!!

The 2006 New AAI Life Members are: Virginia Argeropoulos,cl’53, Ruth Bergmann, cl’50, Lillian Chielewski,cl’48, Lois Doherty,cl’54, Helene Danyow,cl’35 Eleanor Fedell,cl’42, Alice Gosselin,cl’54, Diane Grace,cl’54, Marietta Mahoney,cl’54, Barbara Takacs Powers,cl’54, Elmon Raiford,cl’54 and Beatrice Sandone,cl’54. Congratulations for 50 years of continuous payment of membership dues and may you enjoy many years of Life Membership!!!
